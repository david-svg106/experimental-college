8/28 Nightshades Poisons and Potatos

This website is not affiliated with San Francisco State University

You have all heard of the poisonous nightshades. Plants that cause days of deliarant hallucinations that could be fatal in large doses.
But did you know that potatos, tomatos, bell peppers, spicy peppers, and eggplants are in this "deadly" family?
All these common vegetables produce nicotine!

Today you will handle and hold several of the world's deadliest poisonous plants.
Daturas, brugmansias, and tobaccos.
