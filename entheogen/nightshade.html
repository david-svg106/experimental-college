8/28 Nightshades Poisons and Potatos

This website is not affiliated with San Francisco State University

We've all heard of the poisonous nightshades that can induce days of delusional hallucinations and could be fatal in large doses.
But did you know that potatos, tomatos, bell peppers, spicy peppers, and eggplants are in this "deadly" family? And that they naturally produce nicotine?
