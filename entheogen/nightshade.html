"Poisonous Nightshades". Plants that induce days of deliriant hallucinations; sometimes lethal. Employed in power magic traditions across the world.
Potatos, tomatos, bell peppers, spicy peppers, and eggplants are also in this "deadly" family!
All these common vegetables naturally produce nicotine.

Today you will handle and hold several of Earth's deadliest poisonous plants.
Whether you eat them is up to you. But include us in your will beforehand.

Nicotiana N. tabacum,the tobacco plant commercially sold in cigarettes in cigars.
1,200,000,000 habitual Human users. 7,000,000 annual tobacco-related mortalities.
Completely legal.
N. rustica
Possibly Humanity's first cultivated crop. 9% nicotine content by weight.
Hardened for extreme climates. Traditionally used Datura and Brugmansia
Their natural Tropane Alkaloids are popularly prescribed nausea and seizure suppressant drugs.
Atropine, Scopolamine, and Hyoscyamine. They can also counteract nicotinic nerve agent chemical weapons.
There is no way for you to quantify dosage without analytical instruments. Do not consume these plants.
Branches of Brugmansia sanguinea are used as magic wands.
