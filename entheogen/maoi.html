β-carbolines are a class of Indole Alkaloids. In the brain, DMT is cyclized into the β-carboline Harman.
Many β-carbolines are MonoAmine Oxidase Inhibitors, they Inhibit the Monoamine Oxidase Enzyme which degrades monoamines such as dopamine or serotonin (5-hydroxy-tryptamine.

The "harmala" alkaloids were among the first β-carbolines isolated, from the seeds of Middle-Eastern desert shrub Peganum harmala
The alkaloids are potent MAO inhibitor drugs, and yet widely used by people of the Muslim faith, which usually prohibits drug use.

Ibogaine is chemically similar in appearance to the β-carbolines. It used by the Bwiti religion of Gabon in a several days long rebirth ceremony some call the "Ghost Dance".
One dose of Ibogaine reduces Opiod cravings for up to 6 months. This therapeutic power coupled with fear that this African plant would unify civil rights movements are why was and is so heavily criminalized.
It should be mentioned that Ibogaine may induce seizures or cardiac arrest. Ibogaine clinics exist for people to recieve treatment under guidance of medical professionals.

Peganum harmala, "Esfand / Harmel"
Produces β-carbolines containing seeds. Many of the compounds inhibit the MonoAmine Oxidase enzyme.
Drought-resistant desert plant has spread across the world, with some countries considering it a "Noxious Weed".
This status in California means the seeds can not be legally sold, but they are of deep religion to some adherents of the Muslim faith.
In Iran during the Nourouz new year ceremonies, seeds are thrown into firepits to waft the β-carboline MAOI smoke into the air.
In Pakistan, fathers may swallow up to 10 grams of seeds after dinner to aid in digestion.
Despite this, caution should be taken with these seeds. They prevent your body from destroying waste products.
You could easily unbalance your neurochemistry and feel quite sick and uncomfortable.
Used as an alternative to the endangered ayahuasca vine. Popular for "X-huascas."
While the predominantly harmine-containing ayahuasca vine inhibits psilocin, DMT, or LSD degredation, Peganum harmala's alkaloids appear to also prolong and empower THC, Salvinorin A, and Muscimol. About 3 grams, or one teaspoon, taken one hour before another drug inhibits the body's chemical degradation process.
Thus, people feel subjectively stronger effects that last for a significantly longer amount of time.
